Chae

Son Chae-young, also known by his stage name Chaeyoung, is a rapper and singer from South Korea. She is a part of the JYP Entertainment-created South Korean girl group Twice.




Dahyun

Dahyun is the stage name of South Korean singer and rapper Kim Da-hyun. She is a member of the JYP Entertainment-founded South Korean girl group Twice.
